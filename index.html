2. Box office giants vs fan favorites

Comparing the top 10 by revenue with the top 10 by IMDb score.

There's not a lot of similarities between both charts, but it's worth mentioning that most of the movies with higher scores have low number of reviews due to their niche (Attack on Titan and Solo Leveling have less than 12,000 and 5,000, respectively).

Other movies that are very well reviewed are not American: Maharaja and Amaran, for example, are from India, and both, while having very successful home releases, didn't feature heavily in US theaters, limiting the average IMDb reviewer's exposure to them.

Dune: Part Two is a rare overlap: it appears in both top 10 lists and has the highest number of user ratings in this dataset, making it the most widely rated 2024 release on IMDb.
